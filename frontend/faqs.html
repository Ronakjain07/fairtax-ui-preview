📝 Filing Process

How long does it take to file my ITR with FairTax? ▼

Most returns are filed within 5 minutes of uploading documents. The AI reads your documents instantly, and expert review typically takes a few hours. You'll receive your quote within 24 hours, ready to approve and file.

What documents do I need to upload? ▼

At minimum, you need:

  • Form 16 (if salaried) OR Payslips (if self-employed)
  • Any investment/deduction proofs (home loan certificate, insurance receipts, school fees, etc.)

We also accept:

  • NPS statements
  • Donation receipts (80G)
  • Bank statements (for additional income)
Can I upload documents on mobile phone? ▼

Yes! Our platform is 100% mobile-friendly. You can:

  • Take photos directly from your phone camera
  • Upload PDF files
  • Upload scanned documents

For best results, ensure documents are clear and properly oriented.

Is my data secure on FairTax? ▼

Yes. We use:

  • 256-bit encryption for all data in transit and at rest
  • HTTPS/SSL for secure connections
  • Regular security audits by third-party experts
  • No data sharing with third parties (except required tax authorities)

Your documents are deleted after filing is complete, unless you choose to keep them.

💰 Refunds & Taxes

How much tax refund will I get? ▼

Your refund depends on:

  • Your total income and tax paid
  • Deductions you claim (HRA, investments, charitable donations, etc.)
  • Old Regime vs New Regime comparison

FairTax analyzes both regimes and recommends the one that maximizes your refund. On average, customers save ₹15,000-₹50,000 by switching regimes.

How long will I receive my refund after filing? ▼

Refunds typically arrive in 7-10 working days after filing (standard processing).

If you opt for our Fast Track option, we guarantee expedited processing, and refunds usually come in 3-5 working days (small fee applies).

What if I owe tax instead of getting a refund? ▼

If your income is higher than TDS deducted, you may owe tax. FairTax will clearly show this in your quote. You'll know exactly how much to pay before filing. We help you arrange payment through the income tax portal.

Can I claim exemptions for HRA, LTA, etc.? ▼

Yes! FairTax automatically identifies and claims:

  • HRA (House Rent Allowance) — up to 50% of basic salary
  • LTA (Leave Travel Allowance) — automatic
  • Standard Deduction — ₹50,000 (salaried)
  • Section 80C deductions — insurance, investments, tuition fees, etc.
  • Section 80D — health insurance premiums
