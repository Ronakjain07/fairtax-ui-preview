Filing Process
How long does it take to file my ITR with FairTax?

Most returns are filed within 5 minutes of uploading documents. The AI reads your documents instantly, and expert review typically takes a few hours. You'll receive your quote within 24 hours, ready to approve and file.

What documents do I need to upload?

At minimum, you need:

  • Form 16 (if salaried) or Payslips (if self-employed)
  • Any investment/deduction proofs (home loan certificate, insurance receipts, school fees, etc.)

We also accept NPS statements, donation receipts (80G), and bank statements for additional income sources.

Can I upload documents on a mobile phone?

Yes! Our platform is 100% mobile-friendly. You can take photos directly from your camera, upload PDF files, or upload scanned documents. For best results, ensure documents are clear, well-lit, and properly oriented.

Is my data secure on FairTax?

Absolutely. We use 256-bit encryption for all data in transit and at rest, HTTPS/SSL for secure connections, and regular third-party security audits. Your data is never shared with third parties (except required tax authorities). Documents are deleted after filing is complete, unless you choose to keep them.

Refunds & Taxes
How much tax refund will I get?

Your refund depends on your total income and tax paid, deductions you claim (HRA, investments, donations, etc.), and which tax regime you choose. FairTax analyzes both Old Regime vs New Regime and recommends the one that maximizes your refund. On average, our customers save ₹15,000–₹50,000 by switching regimes.

How long until I receive my refund after filing?

Refunds typically arrive in 7–10 working days after filing (standard processing). If you opt for our Fast Track option, we guarantee expedited processing and refunds usually come in 3–5 working days (small additional fee applies).

What if I owe tax instead of getting a refund?

If your income is higher than the TDS deducted, you may owe tax. FairTax will clearly show this in your quote so you know exactly how much to pay before filing. We guide you through the payment process via the official Income Tax portal.

Can I claim exemptions for HRA, LTA, and other allowances?

Yes! FairTax automatically identifies and claims all eligible deductions:

  • HRA — up to 50% of basic salary (metros), 40% (others)
  • LTA — Leave Travel Allowance, auto-computed
  • Standard Deduction — ₹50,000 for salaried employees
  • Section 80C — insurance, PPF, ELSS, tuition fees & more
  • Section 80D — health insurance premiums

Technical & Support
What file formats does FairTax accept?

We accept PDF (scanned or digital), JPG/JPEG, PNG, BMP, and TIFF image files. Maximum file size is 25 MB per document, and multi-page PDFs are fully supported.

What if the AI fails to extract data from my document?

If the AI cannot read your document, we'll notify you immediately with the reason (e.g., "Image too blurry"). You can then re-scan at higher quality, manually enter the data, or contact our support team for assistance. For best results use clear, well-lit scans with straight orientation.

Can I save my progress and come back later?

Yes! Your progress is automatically saved in your browser session. You'll receive a unique submission ID at the start — keep it safe to resume anytime. Important: clearing your browser cache will delete your progress, so note down your submission ID.
