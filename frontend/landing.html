FAQ

Got questions? We have answers.

Everything you need to know about filing your ITR with FairTax.

For salaried individuals, you need Form 16 from your employer, PAN card, Aadhaar card, bank account details, and investment proofs (Section 80C, 80D, etc.). Our AI can extract most details automatically from Form 16.
The Old Regime allows various deductions (80C, HRA, etc.) reducing your taxable income. The New Regime offers lower slab rates but most deductions are not available. FairTax automatically compares both and tells you which saves you more money.
For most salaried individuals, the process takes as little as 5 minutes. Our AI extracts your data from Form 16, calculates your tax, and our CA team reviews and files within 24 hours. Complex cases (business income, capital gains) may take 2–3 days.
